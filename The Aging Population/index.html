The Aging Population

In 2013, we consumed 120,000 tons worth of clothes and textiles in Sweden.
Which is equal to about 24,000 elephants.

Production and consumption of clothing and textiles requires water consumption, use of chemicals and transportation.

How we consume, reuse and throw away clothes and other textiles affects the climate and environment.

An average swede buys 13 kg new clothes and textiles per person per year.
Which is equal to about 60 new t-shirts or 40 pairs of pants.

Of these clothes 2.4 kg, were recycled in different ways.
Charity in Sweden, charity in other countries, online shopping and second-hand.
8 kg were thrown in the garbage.

Source: SCB
