Can immigration support the Swedish aging population?



Sweden has an aging population which comes with a problem. The workforce does not grow parallel with the growing aging population. The available workforce is decreasing and will have to support more people over time. This is not economically sustainable.

The following graphs show you what would happen to Sweden's workforce without immigration


Over the next coming 30 years from 2015 to 2045 the workforce in Sweden will be reduced by 5% and the non working force will grow with 5% which means a shift of more than 1 million from working force to non working force.




Today there is a lot of people coming to Sweden due to crisis in the world. These people are a real asset for Sweden. Since most of the immigrants coming to Sweden are in a working age they can fill the economic gap that evolves in the workforce when the Swedish population gets older.
